Every product we develop goes through a rigorous process of design, prototyping and testing with professional athletes before reaching the market. Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our products and services.

How long does it take to deliver orders?

Our standard delivery time is 3-5 business days for products in stock. For personalized equipment, the term is 10-15 business days. We offer priority shipping options that can reduce these times by half at an additional cost.

Do you offer programs for clubs and sports teams?

Absolutely! We have special programs for clubs and sports teams that include volume discounts, equipment personalization with the team colors and logo, and a dedicated technical advisor. Contact our institutional sales department for more information.

How does the personalization process work?

The process begins with a detailed consultation where we analyze your specific needs, measurements and preferences. Our design team then creates a virtual prototype that you can review and request modifications. Once approved, we manufacture your personalized equipment with the agreed specifications.

What guarantee do you offer with your products?

All our products have a standard 2-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects and materials. Additionally, we offer an optional 5-year extended warranty. The warranty includes repair or replacement of the product, and in specific cases, technical improvements if the original model has been discontinued.

Do you have physical stores where I can try the products?

We have showrooms in major cities where you can try our products by appointment. In addition, we collaborate with elite training centers where you can experience our equipment under real conditions of use. Consult our contact section to find the nearest point.
