Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Find answers to common queries about Imaginarium AI.

What is Imaginarium AI?

Imaginarium AI is a cutting-edge platform that uses artificial intelligence to help you create virtual worlds quickly and easily. It simplifies the complex process of world design, allowing you to focus on creativity and vision.

Do I need coding experience to use Imaginarium AI?

No coding experience is necessary! Imaginarium AI is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive. You can create stunning worlds with simple text prompts and easy-to-use customization tools.

Can I use Imaginarium AI for commercial projects?

Yes, depending on your chosen plan. Our "Pro" and "Premium" plans include commercial use licenses, allowing you to use the worlds you create for commercial purposes. The "Basic" plan has commercial use as an optional add-on.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer comprehensive support including documentation, tutorials, and a responsive customer support team. "Premium" plan users also receive dedicated priority support and a dedicated account manager.

Is there a free trial available?

Currently, we do not offer a free trial, but we have a "Basic" plan that is very affordable and allows you to explore the core features of Imaginarium AI. We often have promotional periods, so sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about special offers.
